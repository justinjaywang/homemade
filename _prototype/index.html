Pan-seared rainbow trout

'Potlikker' greens and white beans

Trout fillets are dredged lightly in cornmeal and seasoned with paprika and herbs before being pan-seared to a golden hue. Served atop cannellini beans tossed with kale and mustard greens sautéed with bits of ham from the hock and shallots. This protein-rich dish is also a major provider of anti-oxidants and vitamins C, A, & K. Finished with a Southern-inspired potlikker sauce made from braised ham hock, vinegar, and spices.
